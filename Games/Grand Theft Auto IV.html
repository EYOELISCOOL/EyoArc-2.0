Grand Theft Auto: IV

Release: 2008-04-29 Genre: Action, Adventure Developer: Rockstar North Publisher: Rockstar Games

About The Game

Grand Theft Auto IV is a 2008 action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. Set in the fictional Liberty City (based on New York City), the game follows Niko Bellic, a war veteran from Eastern Europe who comes to the United States in search of the American dream, but quickly becomes entangled in the criminal underworld.

GTA IV introduced a more realistic and detailed world than previous entries in the series, with improved physics, a more serious tone, and a complex narrative. The game's "Euphoria" engine allowed for more realistic character movements and interactions with the environment.

The game was critically acclaimed for its storytelling, open world design, and attention to detail. It became one of the most successful entertainment products of all time, selling over 25 million copies worldwide.
